What this prototype proves

Outcome-first travel

Ranking is based on probability of a successful group trip, not just cheapest flights.

Budget-aware scoring

The same city ranks differently for €700 pp and €1,300 pp.

Date sensitivity

July Ibiza and October Ibiza are different products.

Social liquidity

The model rewards places where international conversations happen naturally.
