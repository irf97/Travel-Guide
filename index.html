Scoring model

CEO logic

The winning destination is where budget, travel simplicity, social density, nightlife, and history overlap without forcing the group into taxis every night.

Engineer logic

Score = .20 Budget + .20 Social + .15 Nightlife + .15 History + .15 Travel + .10 Lodging + .05 Demographic Signal

Accommodation law

Central apartment near the social zone usually beats remote villa with pool. Remote villas create taxi friction and reduce spontaneous interactions.

Important concepts

Budget envelopeFlights + lodging should stay under roughly €650 pp.
Social liquidityHow easily strangers become conversations.
Nationality entropyMore mixed tourist origins create broader international social probability.
Villa gravityRemote lodging pulls the group inward and kills spontaneity.
SeasonalityJuly is not the same product as May, September, or October.
